**Sales Financial Analysis Dashboard**
**Project Overview**

The Sales Financial Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize company sales performance, financial health, and customer behavior.
It transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights ‚Äî enabling data-driven decision-making across departments like sales, finance, and operations.

**Dataset Description**

The dataset (sourced from Kaggle) includes comprehensive order-level information covering:

**Category	Fields**
Order Details	Order No, Order Date, Ship Date, Order Priority
Customer Info	Customer Name, Customer Type, Address, City, State, Account Manager
Product Info	Product Name, Product Category, Product Container
Financial Metrics	Cost Price, Retail Price, Profit Margin, Discount %, Discount $, Order Total, Shipping Cost, Total
Operational Data	Ship Mode, Order Quantity, Sub Total

**Objectives**

Analyze total sales, profit margins, and discounts across products and regions

Identify high-value customers and priority orders

Understand sales performance trends over time

Evaluate shipping efficiency and cost distribution

Provide interactive filters (slicers) for customized insights
